04  TONE OF VOICE


The defining line: 'Garmin should pay me commission.' — Confident without arrogant. Irreverent without unprofessional. Reference this before writing anything.
	

4.1  Voice Characteristics
Confident
	Speaks from clinical expertise. Never hedges. No 'might help' or 'could possibly'.
	Direct
	Short sentences. No waffle. Point in the first line.
	Data-first
	Numbers before narrative. '63 → 93' not 'dramatically improved sleep'.
	Irreverent
	Dry wit. Never self-important. Lines people screenshot and share.
	Human
	Sounds like someone who lived this problem.
	Performance
	Always output language. Results, not feelings.
	

4.2  Language — USE
* Drive, focus, productivity — the ICP's own words
* Performance, output, execution, results
* Sleep score, deep sleep, recovery score, HRV — data language the ICP already tracks
* Trenches, eagle view, firing on all cylinders, tired but wired — tribe vocabulary (from real calls)
* Force multiplier, compounds — verbatim from prospects
* Business owners, founders, operators


4.3  Language — NEVER USE
* Wellbeing, wellness, self-care, holistic, balance
* Optimise (marketer voice)
* Natural, organic, gentle
* Transform / transformation (overused)
* Journey (unless grounded in specific client story)
* Hustle (dated)


4.4  Sentence Structure Rules
* Short. Direct. Three words can be a sentence.
* No em dashes in copy. Full stop or restructure.
* Lead with the result. Explain after.
* Data before context: '63 → 93 in one week' not 'a significant improvement from 63 to 93'.
* One idea per sentence in social. Two maximum on website.


4.5  Confirmed Headlines
Primary headline — use on all new pages:


Business owners who are putting in the hours but still feel like they're operating at 60% — I'll get your drive, focus and productivity back using a sleep-driven performance system.
	

Sub-headline — immediately below:


As an NHS Sleep Scientist with an MSc in Sport & Health Science, I don't guess — I use the same diagnostic methodology used in clinical practice, applied to your performance.
	

4.6  Social Proof Language
Say this
	No longer lying in bed awake for hours
	Not this
	Reduced sleep onset latency
	Say this
	No coffee needed after rolling out of bed
	Not this
	Improved morning alertness scores
	Say this
	Performance feels stable again
	Not this
	Demonstrated improvement in cognitive function metrics

07  THE OFFER — SLEEP-DRIVEN PERFORMANCE SYSTEM


System headline: The same output in fewer hours, every week. Not just Mondays.
	

7.1  What It Is
90-day 1:1 coaching system. Not a course. Not a PDF. Not a list of sleep tips. Built around the client's real schedule, real data, and what is actually driving their sleep off track.


7.2  Three Phases
Phase 01 — Stabilise (Weeks 1–4)
Remove the chaos. Make every morning predictable.
* Sleep timing correction and rhythm reset — eliminate the Monday-to-Wednesday cliff
* Caffeine structure audit — stop relying on coffee number three before 9am
* Light exposure and circadian recalibration
* Stress unload system — break the tired-but-wired loop
* Baseline wearable data analysis (Garmin, Whoop, Oura)
Outcome: Predictable mornings. Fewer slow starts. Quick wins.


Phase 02 — Optimise (Weeks 5–8)
Deepen recovery. Fuel the whole week, not just Mondays.
* Deep sleep architecture optimisation — tracked, measurable
* Nutritional timing and energy calibration
* Evening shutdown protocol — stop the 11pm second wind
* Recovery scoring and weekly benchmarking
* Workload-to-energy alignment
Outcome: Consistent energy Monday to Friday. Thursday performing like Monday.


Phase 03 — Sustain (Weeks 9–12)
Make it permanent, especially when life gets hard.
* High-pressure and travel resilience protocols
* Cognitive performance structuring for high-stakes calls and pitches
* Personal long-term performance playbook
* Final 90-day performance data report — measurable proof
* Maintenance framework — new baseline, not a temporary fix
Outcome: High performance under pressure, not just on easy weeks. Permanent.


7.3  What's Included
Initial 1:1 call
	Personalised sleep plan from day one
	Weekly 1:1 coaching
	Focused adjustments based on data
	Daily WhatsApp
	Direct access between sessions
	Wearable data review
	Garmin, Whoop, or Oura — interpreted by a sleep scientist
	Nutrition & exercise guidance
	Sleep does not exist in isolation
	Monthly review calls
	Reassess and optimise
	Final 90-day report
	Data, analysis, and measurable proof of what changed
	

7.4  Who It Is For
* Business owner or founder, 6 months to 3 years in, doing most of it yourself
* Noticed performance fluctuates and suspect sleep is part of it
* Tried things before and nothing has stuck long term
* Already invests in yourself through coaching, training, or personal development
* Wants week-one results, not 6 months of journaling
* Wants a clinical approach, not generic sleep tips


7.5  Who It Is NOT For
* Looking for a quick fix or a magic supplement stack
* Not willing to make small changes to routine
* Thinks 5 hours of sleep is fine and not open to being wrong
* Wants mindset coaching or meditation — this is physiology, not affirmations


7.6  The Urgency Line
Every month you do not address this, the pattern continues. The off days keep tallying up. Sleep does not improve through willpower. It improves through structure.
