Your Sleep Performance Audit

Here's exactly what happens on the call.

1
I find out exactly what's going on for you.

Where the gaps with your sleep are. How long it's been happening. What you've already tried and why it hasn't worked.

2
I tell you what's actually causing it.

The specific reason your sleep is broken, based on your history and your patterns.

3
You leave knowing exactly where you stand.

If I can fix it, I'll walk you through what that looks like. If I can't, I'll tell you that straight and point you in the right direction.

If we're a good fit to work together, I'll explain how that works. If we're not, I'll tell you that too.

Either way you leave the call with more clarity than you came in with.
